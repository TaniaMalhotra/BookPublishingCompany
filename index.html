Welcome To White Cat Publications

A Publishing Company Created By Authors

We are a team of experienced and published writers who have the experience of more than 5 years in the publishing industry. We provide complete guidance to all our clients right from the budding idea of a book, all the way though its designing, manuscripting, and proofreading till its final publishing and distribution. The entire team is very dedicated and committed towards providing value for your money. Choose from a wide range of packages and publish your first solo book for free!! Or, think of collaborating with writers from all over the globe and compile an anthology. We do not have any hidden costs, and work with utmost sincerity and dedication. You will get tired of asking questions, but we won’t be tired of answering your queries.

Join the community of a multitude of writers, poets and authors, and earn with us. We can’t wait to see you on board! Contact us today, and experience a whole new world of publishing.
